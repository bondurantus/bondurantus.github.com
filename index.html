LOREN BONDURANT is an artist and illustrator who works in ink, watercolor and gouache. His work incorporates letterforms and the human figure. Loren has studied drawing, printmaking and calligraphy at Kenyon College, the San Francisco Art Institute, and the Friends of Calligraphy society. He lives in San Francisco, US.

Inquire: lcbondurant@gmail.com.
