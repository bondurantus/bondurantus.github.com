Loren Bondurant is an artist and illustrator who works in ink, watercolor and gouache. His work incorporates letterforms and the human figure. Loren has studied drawing, printmaking and calligraphy at Kenyon College and the San Francisco Art Institute, and with the Friends of Calligraphy society.

He is available for hire for a variety of projects, both large and small. Please contact him at lcbondurant@gmail.com
